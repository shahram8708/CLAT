Privacy Policy

Career Launcher Ahmedabad is committed to protecting your personal information and handling it responsibly.

1. Introduction and Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the website, admission processes, classroom and online learning systems, scholarship registration, counselling interactions, and payment workflows operated by Career Launcher Ahmedabad. By using our services, you consent to data processing as described in this policy.

2. What Data We Collect

We may collect personal and academic information including your full name, phone number, email address, city, exam preference, preparation interests, and counselling notes. For enrolled students we may also collect enrollment records, attendance indicators, test performance, scholarship details, and communication history. When payments are made, transaction level information is processed through authorized payment gateways.

We also collect technical usage data such as browser type, device information, referral source, session pages, and interaction logs to improve website and student experience.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for student onboarding, demo scheduling, course enrollment, scholarship administration, communication on classes and tests, payment confirmation, support resolution, and service improvements. We may also use data for legal compliance, fraud prevention, and record maintenance required for coaching operations.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Data may be shared with CL Educate Ltd. network entities only where required for academic operations, centralized support, or program coordination. For online fee collection, payment details are processed securely by Razorpay and related banking networks under their own regulated compliance frameworks.

5. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and analytics tools to remember preferences, understand user journeys, and improve performance. You can control cookies from browser settings, but disabling them may affect certain website features.

6. Data Retention Policy

Prospect and student data is retained for as long as needed for admission support, academic delivery, legal obligations, and financial recordkeeping. Data that is no longer required is deleted or anonymized according to operational and legal requirements.

7. Your Rights under Indian Law

In line with the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you may request access, correction, update, or deletion of your personal data subject to lawful limitations. You may also withdraw consent for non essential communications where applicable.

8. Contact for Data Requests

For privacy requests, corrections, or grievance redressal, contact us at cl_ahmedabad@careerlauncher.com or call +91 9978559986. You may also write to us at Career Launcher Ahmedabad, A 102, Karmyog Heights, Navrangpura, Ahmedabad 380009.
