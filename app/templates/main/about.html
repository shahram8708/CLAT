About Career Launcher Ahmedabad

A trusted coaching destination in Ahmedabad with a 30+ year legacy of building top performers across CAT, CLAT, IPMAT, CUET and beyond.

Institute Overview

Career Launcher Ahmedabad was built with one purpose, to give ambitious students in Gujarat access to structured mentoring, best in class faculty, and exam specific strategy that delivers measurable outcomes. Over the years, the centre has become a trusted name for CAT, CLAT, IPMAT, GMAT, CUET and foundational mathematics training.

As part of the nationwide Career Launcher ecosystem, the Ahmedabad centre combines local mentorship with national academic infrastructure. Students receive classroom discipline, digital learning access, adaptive mock analysis, and guided mentoring so that preparation remains consistent from day one till exam day.

Located in Navrangpura, the centre serves school students, graduates, and working professionals through flexible classroom, online and hybrid pathways. The focus remains practical and outcome driven, with continuous tracking, doubt support, and performance correction at every stage.

CL National Brand

Career Launcher is one of India’s most recognized education brands, founded by an IIT-IIM alumni team and backed by CL Educate Ltd. The national network supports students across MBA, Law, BBA, Study Abroad, and School preparation verticals through integrated classroom and technology platforms.

The Ahmedabad centre operates under the same high standards for curriculum design, assessment quality, and mentoring discipline. Students benefit from national test benchmarking, centralized study resources, and a mature academic framework that has evolved through decades of exam insights. CL learners also engage with pathways connected to leading NAAC accredited universities and institutions across India.

The CL model emphasizes conceptual clarity, strategic exam approach, and personalized intervention. This blend of national capability and local execution gives Ahmedabad students a strong and reliable preparation pathway.
